Jim Thornton is an American radio and television announcer best known for his anchoring work on all-news radio KNX 1070. Likewise, he earns recognition for his voiceover works on different video games, movies, and television shows.

Jim Thornton Job, Salary & Net Worth

As a radio and television announcer plus voice actor, Jim Thornton enjoys a commanding professional salary. As an anchor from the Los Angeles area, Jim’s salary swirls around $59k per year. Besides, his earnings as a voice actor take his net worth past $1 million.

Jim commenced his broadcasting career in 1985, a year after he moved to Los Angeles. Soon, he became known as the afternoon anchor on all-news radio KNX 1070 in Los Angeles. Prior to the promotion, he served as a traffic reporter.
