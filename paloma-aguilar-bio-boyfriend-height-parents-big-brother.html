Get To Know Big Brother Paloma Aguilar! Bio, Boyfriend, Height, Parents

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, August 30, 2024

Paloma Aguilar is one of the 16 cast members of Big Brother 24. We are two episodes in and viewers have already dubbed Paloma as the villain of the show. Sharing her strategy with US Weekly, Paloma shared that she will go for the big players.

Paloma added that she thinks it’s sad because those players are the ones who she has the best connections with but she thinks they have to be out if she wants to have a chance to wind the show. She added that she would bring her no.1 ally in the finals so that it would be a way better story to tell if she brings someone evenly matched with her.

Paloma shared that she watched Big Brother US and Canada and she completed watching season 6 of Big Brother Canada. Some of her favorite contestants are Tyler C from season 20. She thinks they have many things in common like showing leadership and being carefree in the house.

Learn more about Paloma Aguilar’s parents, job, age, birthday, and other details.

Paloma Aguilar On Big Brother 24

As for Paloma Aguilar’s current journey in Big Brother 24 (till episode 2), she has been chosen by Backstage Boss Joe “Pooch” Pucciarelli as one of three contestants alongside Alyssa Snider and Brittany Hoopes to join Pooch backstage. She also turned all the attention towards herself when she joined the alliance together dubbed “Girls’ Girls”.

Paloma commented about fellow contestant Taylor Hale saying, “My initial impression of Taylor is that she wants to use her beauty to allure men which is a strategy which is like so old”. She added, “Her demeanor comes across as even more cocky when she’s not trying to make an effort with women. She’s just digging that hole for herself with the girls”.

Paloma was also communicating with this week’s HOH, Daniel Durston to put Taylor as a possible eviction choice. “She’s a question mark,” Paloma starts. “She’s one of the people that I didn’t connect with immediately,” Daniel admits. “I just don’t know if I can trust her,” Paloma adds.

“Taylor is just not part of the Girls’ Girls alliance and she’s spending so much time around the guys. I wouldn’t personally go for Taylor because if she somehow stays in the house, she’s gonna come for me…so of course, I want to persuade [Daniel] to help my game,” Paloma says in a talking head.

“Even though Daniel hasn’t nominated Taylor, seeing two guys on the block is wonderful for Paloma’s game because of numbers,” Paloma says after the nominee reveal. “The only thing I’m worried about is this backstage fiasco…you never know what’s in store.”

Does Paloma Aguilar Have A Boyfriend?

Paloma Aguilar appears happily single. When it comes to her personal life, she chooses to keep everything away from the eyes of the public and social media.

Paloma Aguilar Job

Paloma Aguilar interned at GRACED BY GRIT Foundation as a sales supervisor & marketing intern from 2016 to 2018. In June 2018, she joined Nordstorm in sales & logistics and worked till August 2018. From June 2018 to September 2018, she worked at Happier Daily Podcast Launch Coordinator.

Two months later, Paloma joined Teva Pharmaceuticals in San Diego as an Administrative Intern.

Paloma also delved into broadcast journalism working for CalTV from December 2019 to July 2021. For six months in 2021, she worked at Dreamstyle Remodeling as a Designer Sales Consultant.

In 2022, she is currently working as a design consultant and home remodeler. She is looking to build a career in Real Estate & Interior Design.

Furthermore, Paloma also promotes models for clothing and apparel brands such as N Ü Ξ R A Swim, Free People, and Kate Spade to name a few. She loves flaunting her stunning body on her Instagram. Moreover, she likes to indulge in yoga and fitness and spend time on the beach.

Paloma attended the University of California, Berkeley, and graduated in 2021 studying Media Studies and Political Economy. She completed her high school at San Marcos High in 2017. While there she was on the track & field team.

Paloma Aguilar Parents

Paloma Aguilar was born to her mother Georgina Kyriazis and her father Vincent Aguilar.

Born in June 1974, her mother is 48 years old. Georgina is of Greek-American ethnicity. She has a sister named Laura Kyriazis.

As for her father, Vincent was born in December 1967. He is 54 years old in 2022. He hailed from Mexico.

Hence Paloma is Mexican-Greek-American by ethnicity.

By profession, Georgina is currently working at Neurocrine Biosciences as a Neuro Psych Account Specialist.

In addition, Georgina is also working at Teva Pharmaceuticals as an Executive Sales Representative. Before that, she worked for Sanofi and Animas Corporation as a sales representative and business manager.

Georgina graduated from UC San Diego with a degree in Communications, Business/Corporate Communication in 1999.

Before coming to the show, Paloma told Parade that her father was a psychologist. Because of that, she thought that she has a certain edge in that aspect. Vincent is a marriage & family therapist, LMFT. He began his professional career graduating with a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from the University of San Francisco and forged his path to becoming an accomplished Marketing Executive in Latin America, for such prominent companies as Georgia Pacific and Old Castle Distribution.

Vincent found a hidden purpose that lies in treating and empowering the people suﬀering from trauma, depression, anxiety, organic mental illnesses, addictions, and interpersonal relational problems.

Vincent completed his master of art in Psychology from Chapman University.
