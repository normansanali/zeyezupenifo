Lewis Tan | Tattoo Meaning | Parents And Ethnicity

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, July 14, 2024

Lewis Tan, a well-known actor, has several tattoos on his body, particularly his hands and arms. He reportedly remarked that his designs functioned like a journal and told his narrative.

Lewis Singwah Tan, an English actor and martial artist best known for playing Lu Xin Lee in Netflix’s first-ever Asian-American series Wu Assassins, was born Lewis Tan.

The entertainer, who was born in England, has been working in the business since 1990. Lewis has acted in several films and television shows during his more than three-decades-long profession.

His significant productions include Into the Badlands, Deadpool 2, Shadow and Bone, and Mortal Kombat. Lewis Tan is well known for his tattoos in addition to his acting career. In today’s little article, let’s find out about his tattoos and their significance.

Lewis Tan Tattoo Meaning And Designs, Shadow and Bone

Lewis Tan, the star of Shadow and Bone, has several little tattoos.

The actor acknowledged having several tattoos, which are a part of his biography, in an interview with Cosmopolitan published in April 2021. “I have many tattoos; they are like a diary to me; they tell my story.”

The 36-year-old celebrity said that his ex-girlfriend’s handwriting appears on his fingers where “grace” and “love” are written. The performer claimed that while he and his previous partner had split, they remained close friends. Therefore, the tattoos stand for that emotion.

Lewis Tan has how many tattoos?

Unknown is the actual amount of tattoos the actor has on his body. However, he has a sizable number of them, and they keep multiplying. Based on the Morgana Ravens 2021 study, some of them are mentioned below.

2 Head and Neck

Lewis Tan’s Parents’ Race

Lewis Tan Singwah Tan was born in Salford, England, on February 4th, 1987. He was raised in Los Angeles. His parents are Philip Tan and Joanne Cassidy, respectively.

Lewis is the oldest of his parents’ four children. Sam Tan, his brother, is also an actor. Additionally, Evan, his youngest sibling, is said to be a photographer.

Lewis Tan’s mother is a former British fashion model, while his father is a well-known actor. His father is a martial artist and stunt coordinator who is Chinese and Singaporean. The Mortal Kombat actor spent his formative years in China, Spain, and France.

When his father was employed as a fight coordinator for Tim Burton’s 1989 movie Batman, his family moved to Los Angeles. The actor started practicing at an early age since his father is a martial artist. In his early adolescent years, he also began his theater training.
