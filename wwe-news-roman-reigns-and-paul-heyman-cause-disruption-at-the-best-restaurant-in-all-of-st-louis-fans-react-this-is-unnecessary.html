Roman Reigns and Paul Heyman Cause Disruption at the Best Restaurant in All ofSt. Louis, Fans React

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, September 1, 2024

Roman Reigns has been a force to reckon with ever since his heel turn in 2020. Many have tried knocking him off his perch but have failed miserably. On top of that, Reigns’ pairing with ‘The Wise Man’ Paul Heyman has turned out to be a masterstroke for the WWE. Even when they are off the screen they are never far off from their epic personas.

The Tribal Chief picks and chooses his appearances at WWE televised shows nowadays. He was present at last night’s RAW episode, which took place in St. Louis.

And before an appearance on TV, Heyman gave a glimpse of what Reigns was up to in the preceding afternoon. The notorious manager tweeted the image of an empty restaurant. The caption for the post read, “When the #TribalChief @WWERomanReigns wants to eat lunch, you shut down the best restaurant in all of #StLouis to ACKNOWLEDGE him!” 

Fans react to Paul Heyman’s social media post on Reigns

This post met its intended purpose as netizens took to social media in order to react to it. While some were appreciative of the fact that Reigns had the restaurant all by himself some displayed their apprehensions.

Whether the photograph was taken at an opportune moment by The Bloodline’s manager  or he was just speaking the facts is not known. It is definitely open to the interpretation of the spectators and they just did that. While Heyman’s acted as Reigns’ mouthpiece on Twitter. The Head of The Table did not need his aid at the red brand’s latest episode. He held his own while confronting his WrestleMania 39 opponent Cody Rhodes.

Romans Reigns spewed verbal venom on RAW

Reigns went face-to-face with The Second Generation superstar for only the second time. But he did an impressive job on the mic blurring the lines between reality and kayfabe.

The undisputed WWE Universal Champion mentioned Rhodes’s career in AEW albeit without naming the Jacksonville-based promotion.

He said, “You didn’t want to do the Stardust thing, so you ran away. Then you started a company that you couldn’t get over in and you ran away”. The enticing promo went a long way in hyping up their epic showdown on April 2nd.

Paul Heyman has shown his managerial wits whenever he has been paired with an able wrestler. And Roman Reigns has certainly come to his own in the past few years. The duo does an incredible job, with Heyman hyping up Reigns on every possible occasion whether inside or outside the ring. And The Tribal Chief working his magic on the mic and in the ring.
