Wiki/Biography

Kishori Godbole was born on Wednesday, 15 December 1976 (age 45 years; as of 2021) in Nashik, Maharashtra, India. Her zodiac sign is Sagittarius. Kishori studied till matriculation at Parle Tilak Vidyalaya English Medium School, Mumbai, India. Later, she studied at Marathi VidyaNiketan High School, Belagavi, Karnataka. Kishori Godbole completed her graduation in Action Management at the University of Mumbai.

Career

Film

In 1999, Kishori Godbole made her debut in Bollywood with the Hindi film Kohram in which she played the role of Bharti.

Poster of Kishori Godbole debut film Kohram

In 2002, she was seen in the film Ek Aur Visphot as Kiran Joshi. In 2005, Kishori appeared in the Marathi film Khabardaar in which she played the role of Tulsi. In 2006, she played the role of Naina Desai in the Marathi comedy film Majha Navra Tujhi Bayko. In 2007, she appeared in the Marathi film Baba Lagin. In 2008, Kishori was seen in the Marathi Comedy film Full 3 Dhamaal as Kishori Kulkarni.

Poster of Kishori Godbole’s Marathi film Full 3 Dhamaal

In 2011, she appeared in the Marathi film One Room Kitchen in which she played the role of Neha.

Poster of Kishori Godbole’s film One Room Kitchen

Television

In 1997, Kishori Godbole made her television debut in the  Zee TV show Chutki Bajake.

Kishori Godbole in a still from her show Chutki Bajake

In the same year, Kishori played various characters in the StarPlus show ‘Rin Ek Do Teen’ as the show made parodies (spoofs) of popular Hindi movies. In 1999, she appeared in the show Hudd Kar Di in which she played the role of Shobha on Zee TV. In 2004, she was seen in the Zee Marathi show Adhuri Ek Kahani in which she played the role of Amruta Vijay Deodhar.

Kishori Godbole in a still from her show Adhuri Ek Kahani

In the same year, she appeared in the Sony TV show Ye Meri Life Hai in which she played the role of Prerna. In 2008, she participated in the Zee Marathi comedy show, Fu Bai Fu. In 2011, she hosted the show Happy Housewives Club on Sony SAB. In the same year, Kishori appeared in the show Mrs Tendulkar as Mrs Vibhavari Suhaas Tendulkar on Sony SAB.

The poster of Kishori Godbole’s show Mrs Tendulkar

In 2013, Kishori played the role of Mrs Maya Amitabh Raje in the Marathi television show Madhuri Middle Class on Star Pravah. In 2016, she was seen in the show Khidki in which she played the role of Kalavati on Sony SAB. In 2019, she appeared in the show Mere Sai-Shraddha Aur Saburi as Baija Maa on Sony TV.

Kishori Godbole in a still from her show Mere Sai-Shraddha Aur Saburi
