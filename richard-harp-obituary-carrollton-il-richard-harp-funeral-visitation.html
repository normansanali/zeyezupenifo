Richard Harp Obituary Carrollton IL, Richard Harp Funeral &amp; Visitation

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 17, 2024

Richard Harp Obituary, Death Cause – Carrollton mourns the passing of Richard Harp, a beloved member of the community who touched the lives of many. At the age of 62, Richard Harp left this world on December 9, 2023, at Barnes-Jewish Hospital. As friends and family gather to celebrate his life, the community reflects on the legacy he leaves behind.

Born and raised in Carrollton, Richard Harp was a fixture in the community. He was known for his warm personality, generosity, and unwavering commitment to his family and friends. As a tribute to his memory, a visitation has been arranged at Crawford Funeral Home on Monday, December 18, from 4-8 p.m. This will provide an opportunity for those who knew Richard to come together, share memories, and offer support to one another during this difficult time.

The funeral service will take place the following day at 11 a.m. at Lone Oak Golf Course, a place that held special significance for Richard. As friends and family gather at this beautiful venue, they will have the chance to celebrate a life well-lived. The service will be a time to remember the joy and love Richard brought to the community, as well as an opportunity to find solace in shared memories.

Following the funeral, Richard Harp will be laid to rest at Manchester Cemetery, a serene and peaceful location that will serve as a fitting final resting place for a man who brought so much peace and joy to those around him. The burial will be a private moment for the family to say their final goodbyes and find comfort in the knowledge that Richard’s memory will live on in the hearts of those who knew and loved him.

In times of sorrow, the Carrollton community has shown its strength and unity. As neighbors and friends come together to support the Harp family, the outpouring of love and compassion is a testament to the impact Richard had on the lives of those around him. The community’s presence during the visitation and funeral services will serve as a source of comfort for the grieving family, reinforcing the idea that no one is alone in their grief.

Richard Harp’s passing leaves a void in the hearts of those who had the privilege of knowing him. As the community gathers to celebrate his life and bid him a fond farewell, they do so with a mixture of sadness and gratitude for the memories shared. Through the tears and laughter, Richard Harp’s legacy will endure, a testament to a life well-lived in service to others.
