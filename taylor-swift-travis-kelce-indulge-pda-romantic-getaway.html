Taylor Swift And Travis Kelce Shared Some PDA While On Vacation In The Bahamas

Despite being the most popular celebrity couple in the world right now, Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce are not known for indulging in PDA that often. They have been seen kissing on a few occasions, like after the Kansas City Chiefs won the SuperBowl, or after some of Taylor Swift's concerts, but this is a whole other level.

Recently, it became known that the couple escaped on a romantic getaway to the Bahamas for a few days. They are already back home, but now some photos have been leaked from their time on vacation, and they are steamy.

In the new pictures, the couple are in their bathing suits and all over each other. They walked into the water and held each other close, sitting in the shallows, and after a while they were spotted making out.

After they returned to the shore, the couple dried off and lay down to sunbathe, still touching each other, and at one point Travis Kelce leaned over to squeeze Taylor Swift's backside.

According to a source, they stayed in the Bahamas for "several days", and had the time of their lives. "It was a much-needed break for both of them," the source added. "They very much enjoyed it. They are so happy together."

Taylor And Travis Are Planning On Spending Their Time Off Together

Despite being back home, Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce are still planning on spending a lot of time together in their downtime. Now that football season is over and the Eras tour is on a break until May, the couple are making the best of the time they have without rushing. While they still saw each other while they were working (Taylor attended Travis' games and he went with her on tour sometimes), it's not the same as being with each other full time.

According to an insider, they are both happy to have "a break from the buzz of their careers."
