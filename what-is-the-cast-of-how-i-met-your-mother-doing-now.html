What Is The Cast Of How I Met Your Mother Doing Now?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, July 14, 2024

How I Met Your Mother was a sitcom that centered around Ted Mosby rehashing the story of how he met the mother of his two children, while also detailing other significant moments in his life. Combining the wacky hijinks humor of The Office with the friendship dynamic of Friends, HIMYM lasted 9 seasons and was the recipient of countless award nominations. But, what is the cast of the popular sitcom currently up to these days?

The unfortunate passing of the show’s narrator Bob Saget and the controversial finale has left a few less than fond memories of the series in the minds of some fans. However, despite the setbacks, the series is still beloved by fans and hasn't stopped the momentum of the cast’s careers. At any rate, let’s take a look at what the gang from McClaren’s has been up to since the end of the show, shall we?

8 David Henrie

David Henrie played Ted Mosby’s son Luke. Along with sister Penny (played by Lyndsy Fonseca), Luke was featured (primarily) at the beginning of each episode, usually seeming rather uninterested. Appearing on TV as early as 2002, Henrie was featured in dramas such as NCIS and comedies the likes of That’s So Raven before being cast in HIMYM. Post-HIMYM saw the Grown Ups 2 actor continue to pick up projects for film and TV, with his most recent film being the biopic Reagan. Playing the part of the young adult version of the former president, the film is set to debut in 2023 and stars Dennis Quaid as Reagan in his elder years. Unfortunately, in 2018, Henrie was involved in an incident at LAX where the actor was arrested and charged with carrying a firearm.

7 Lyndsy Fonseca

Lyndsy Fonseca portrayed Ted’s daughter Penny who, along with her brother, is featured at the beginning of every episode as older Ted (played by the late Bob Saget) tells the tale. Before being cast as Penny Mosby, Fonseca was featured in a slew of TV series such as The Young and the Restless as well as films, most notably the Kick-Ass series. After HIMYM wrapped, the Boston Public actress was cast in the MCU series Agent Carter, playing Angie Martinelli on the small screen. Over in the film world, Fonseca I set to be featured in the upcoming biographical film Spinning Gold.

6 Cristin Milioti

At the end of season 8, the world was first introduced to Tracy, Ted’s love interest and the mother that the show’s plot revolved around. Fans loved the character, but hated the fate that befell her. Cristin Milioti brought Tracy to life and managed to please fans with only a small amount of screen time in the final season. Milioti appeared in high-profile shows such as The Sopranos and films such as 2012’s Sleepwalk with Me long before showing up on HIMYM. After the show’s finale, Milioti lent her voice to Family Guy, The Simpsons and The Venture Bros. Milioti was also featured in the anthology series Black Mirror. Most recently, The Wolf of Wall Street actress starred alongside Andy Samberg in Palm Springs and starred in two TV series in 2021-22, Made For Love and The Resort.

5 Josh Radnor

Ted Mosby himself Josh Radnor started his acting career appearing in the short-lived sitcom Welcome to New York. Radnor then found small parts in films such as Not Another Teen Movie before transitioning to the stage, performing in The Graduate among other theater projects. After starring in HIMYM, Radnor would star in the 2018 musical drama Rise, which only lasted a single season. Radnor made a return to the stage, starring as Seymour in Little Shop of Horrors. Aside from his acting projects, Radnor also has an indie folk band called Radnor and Lee with Ben Lee.

4 Alyson Hannigan

With roles such as Willow in Buffy the Vampire Slayer and the infamous role of Michelle Flaherty in the American Pie series, Alyson Hannigan was well established long before appearing as Lily on HIMYM. However, after being cast in the hit series, Hannigan became more popular than ever. After the conclusion of the show, Hannigan would make appearances in several popular shows, including Kim Possible and as recently as 2021, Hannigan co-hosted the comedy clip show Adorableness. On the big screen, Hannigan starred in the 2021 family comedy-drama Flora and Ulysses.

3 Jason Segel

Jason Segel portrayed lovable lawyer and Ted Mosby’s best friend Marshall on HIMYM. Segel was featured in many comedies in the early to late 2000s, such as Forgetting Sarah Marshall and I Love You, Man, but got his start on the teen comedy Freaks and Geeks (1999-2000.) After HIMYM ended, Segel would be featured in a slew of big screen films, such as Sex Tape with Cameron Diaz and his latest being the 2022 crime thriller Windfall. Segel also starred in the Apple TV+ coming of age drama The Sky Is Everywhere in 2022.

2 Cobie Smulders

Cobie Smulders experienced minor success with roles in Tru Calling and Smallville before landing the role of Robin on HIMYM. Smulders would end up finding herself within the mega franchise that is the MCU, shortly before the concussion of HIMYM, portraying Maria Hill in an ongoing role. As Maria Hill, Smulders would appear in many MCU films, as well as Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D on the small screen. Outside the MCU, Smulders starred in the 2020 romantic drama Cicada.

1 Neil Patrick Harris

Neil Patrick Harris first broke on the small screen as teenage doctor phenom Doogie Howser back in the late '80s-early '90s. Harris experienced a bit of a career stall after Doogie Howser: MD concluded, appearing steadily in minor roles until experiencing a small screen career revival when he was cast as Barney on HIMYM. After the show, Harris continued to work on the stage, most recently in Into the Woods, film such as The Matrix Resurrections, and is set to appear in Doctor Who.
