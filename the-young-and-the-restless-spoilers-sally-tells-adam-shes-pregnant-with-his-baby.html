The Young and the Restless Spoilers: Sally Tells Adam Shes Pregnant With His Baby?

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 25, 2024

The Young and the Restless (Y&R) spoilers tease that Sally Spectra (Courtney Hope) will tell Adam Newman (Mark Grossman) that he’s her baby’s father.

Sally is reeling after just learning that she’s pregnant. She knows she slept with both Adam and Nick Newman (Joshua Morrow), which makes it complicated to know who fathered her unborn child.

The Young and the Restless spoilers say that Sally discovers that Adam is the baby’s father based on her fertile days.

On the days she could get pregnant, she wasn’t with Nick. However, smack dab in the middle of her fertile days, she was with Adam.

Knowing that, she concludes that Adam is the child’s father.

When Sally meets with Adam at the coffee shop, she questions why he’s nice to her despite the fact she moved on with Nick.

Y&R spoilers state that Adam will tell her that she knows the answer to that.

Sally knows that Adam wants to be with her. He’s made his position clear.

Adam’s felt depressed about their breakup and can’t move on. Adam doesn’t think their relationship is over.

Y&R spoilers reveal that when Sally confesses she’s pregnant, Adam immediately thinks he’s the father.

Adam doesn’t think Nick could be the father, suggesting she stop the madness and give their relationship another chance.

Sally will feel conflicted about Adam. She loves him and knows he cares for her.

However, Sally doesn’t want to put herself in a position for him to hurt her. And now, she has a baby to consider.

Now that Adam believes he fathered Sally’s baby, he will up the ante.

It won’t be long before Nick knows about the pregnancy and expresses his doubts about Sally’s child’s paternity.

Sally and Adam will inevitably reunite, but it could take a little while. The pregnancy will bring them closer, which will create distance from Nick.

The Young and the Restless spoilers say that Adam will promise to be there for Sally, suggesting they should get back together.

It will complicate an already highly complex situation.
