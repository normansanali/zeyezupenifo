Peter Andre Net Worth: How Much Is Peter Andre Worth?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, September 10, 2024

Peter Andre net worth-British singer and TV personality, Peter James Andre was born on February 27, 1973, in Harrow, London in the United Kingdom.

How much is Peter Andre worth?

Peter Andre has a net worth estimated to be about $22 million as of 2023. He has made a successful career as a singer and television personality and lives a luxurious life from the money that he has created from his career. He has signed a big contract with the record label for singing.

Peter Andre career

Andre, a 16-year-old competitor on the Australian talent show New Faces in 1989, was given a $146,000 recording contract as a result of his participation. During this time, Andre attended Benowa State High School on the Gold Coast while residing in a Surfers Paradise apartment near the ocean.

Andre was flown to the UK to record his first song, “Drive Me Crazy,” and his breakout hit, “Gimme Little Sign,” at PWL Studios with producers Phil Harding and Ian Curnow.

Andre released “Drive Me Crazy” in 1992, and it reached a high position of No. 72 on the Australian singles chart. His second single, “Gimme Little Sign,” which peaked at No. 3 and stayed a total of 31 weeks in the charts, marked his musical breakthrough, nevertheless.

Andre won an ARIA Award in 1993 for having the best-selling Australian single of the year. On September 30, 1996, he released his second studio album, which peaked at No. 1 on the UK Albums Chart.

The album featured the smash song “Mysterious Girl,” which first appeared in 1996 and debuted at number two on the UK Singles Chart before being re-released at number one in 2004 in response to Andre’s participation in I’m a Celebrity…Get Me Out of Here! Andre eventually left for the UK in the middle of the 1990s.

The sixth studio album by Peter, Revelation, was released on September 14th, 2009. The album reached at No. 3 while notable songs “Behind Closed Doors” and “Unconditional” peaked at Nos. 4 and 50 in the UK charts, respectively.

Andre and Katie Price started the Katie & Peter brand on ITV2 while they were still married, which featured episodes that highlighted their relationship.

When Jordan Met Peter, Jordan & Peter: Laid Bare, and Jordan & Peter: Marriage and Mayhem (2004–2005); Katie & Peter: The Next Chapter, Katie & Peter: The Baby Diaries, and Katie & Peter: Unleashed (all 2007); Katie & Peter: Down Under; and Katie & Peter: African Adventures (all 2008); and Katie & Peter: Stateside in 2009; were among the franchise’s fly-on-the-wall reality series.

Their respective series were taped as a result of their 2009 breakup: Peter Andre: The Next Chapter aired on ITV2 till 2011; it was then followed by Peter Andre: Here 2 Help (2011) and Peter Andre: My Life (2011–2013).

On the ITV show Odd One in July 2010, Andre and Jason Manford served as team captains. Andre appeared as a guest judge on the ITV entertainment program Your Face Sounds Familiar in 2013 and afterward presented five Sunday Scoop episodes as a guest.

Since November 11 of that year, Andre has hosted Peter Andre’s 60 Minute Makeover, a rebranded version of 60 Minute Makeover.

As the lead single from his album Big Night, Andre debuted the song “Kid” (for the movie Mr. Peabody & Sherman) in 2014. Andre and Jamelia co-hosted the ITVBe opening on October 8 of that same year.

Andre started appearing in the television ads for the Iceland grocery store in 2014. Since then, Andre has acted in the ITV weekly program Give a Pet a Home, which collaborates with the Birmingham RSPCA.

He was announced as a contender for the thirteenth season of Strictly Come Dancing in August 2015, and the season premiered a month later with him partnered with Janette Manrara. They were eliminated in week 10, just before the quarterfinal and hence placed seventh.

In the Thomas & Friends production Big World! Big Adventures from 2018 to 2020, Andre provided the voice of Ace the race car.
