Rihanna Reveals the Cutest Thing Her Baby Son Does Every Morning

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, July 28, 2024

There is no shortage of cherished moments between new mom Rihanna and her baby son, whom she welcomed this past May with boyfriend A$AP Rocky.

In a new interview with Entertainment Tonight, the multi-hyphenate opened up about embracing motherhood and revealed her favorite part about spending time with her newborn.

"Oh my god, the mornings, like, seeing his morning face!" she told the outlet on the red carpet for the premiere of her Savage x Fenty Show Vol. 4. "Seeing a baby with, like, little bags and waking up and they're just, like, startled. They're trying to figure out where they're at. It's the cutest, it's my favorite part of the day."

She added that she often sings to her child and will even occasionally "freestyle" songs to him. "He's amazing, he's a happy baby," she said.

Being a mother has also influenced her outlook as a fashion and business mogul.

"You know, I always thought doing kid stuff would be cliche and the obvious thing to do when you have a baby. It's, like, the obvious next step," she said. "But when I went shopping, that's when I realized like, OK, maybe I need to start making the stuff that I want my kids to wear because it's hard! I got my own ideas."

Rihanna has recently been sharing more and more details about her personal life since giving birth to her baby boy.

Of her upcoming, highly anticipated Super Bowl halftime performance, she said in an interview, "If I'm going to leave my baby, I'm going to leave my baby for something special. It was now or never for me [for doing the Super Bowl halftime show]."
