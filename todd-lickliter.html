Biography

Todd Lickliter was born in Indiana on April 17, 1955. Named the 2007 NABC National Coach of the Year after leading the Butler University Bulldogs’ basketball team to a Horizon League Championship. He then went on to coach at Iowa from 2007 to 2010. He served as a mentor for Brad Stevens, who took over at the helm of Butler after he left. He played his college ball at Central Florida CC for a year before transferring to Butler University.
