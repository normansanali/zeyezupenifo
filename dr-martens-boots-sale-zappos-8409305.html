Blake Lively Wears These Classic Biker Boots, and I Found 7 Lookalikes

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, August 24, 2024

When the cold weather rolls around, there’s one shoe style that people can’t get enough of: boots. And there are seemingly endless trendy styles to add to your wardrobe. If you’re into an edgier look, then you can’t go wrong with combat boots. The classic biker boots will never go out of style — just look at your favorite stars. Taylor Swift has worn them on repeat this fall, and Blake Lively recently stepped out in an everyday pair from the tried-and-true brand Dr. Martens — so naturally, I now need a pair of Dr. Martens. 

Dr. Martens is probably one of the first brands to come to mind when shopping for combat boots, and for good reason. Not only do the sleek shoes make outfits look effortlessly cool, but they're made to last (even through trends). In fact, I still wear my pair of Dr. Martens from six years ago, and I’m not the only one — there must be a reason that other stars like Selena Gomez, Gigi Hadid, and Olivia Rodrigo wear the brand too, right?

If you’re curious to see what all the hype is about, you’re in luck. I found over 100 styles of Dr. Martens shoes marked down for Zappos’ Cyber Week sale. Rather than scour through the site yourself, take a look below at a few of my favorites that are up to 43 percent off right now. 

7 On-Sale Dr. Martens Boots at Zappos

Combs Leather Boots

The Dr. Martens Combs Leather Boots are a close match to Blake Lively’s pair. It comes in a dark brown leather that delivers an identical rugged look. Plus, these universally flattering boots feature a lightweight sole that offers great traction and stability. Not to mention, they’re so “comfortable right out of the box,” according to shoppers. 

1460 W Boots

Classic Dr. Martens black boots are always a foolproof choice. But if you want to add a subtle twist, try the brand’s 1460 W boots that come in a glossy shine. The patent leather makes the shoes look brand new with each wear. The best part? They’re 25 percent off, so snag on sale ASAP.

Don’t just take our word for it — shoppers love these kicks. “I am almost 70 years old and these still hold their appeal,” said one reviewer. “I wore them a long time ago and have returned to them because they have that feeling of youth and rebelliousness. To hell with age-appropriate!”

Serena 8-Eye Boots

Keep your feet toasty warm with Dr. Martens’ Serena 8-Eye Boots. The on-sale combat boots contain a soft, faux fur lining that’s a must-have for winter. Reviewers agree, saying “most comfortable boots ever and warm,” adding, “[they’re] perfect for the fall and winter.” What’s more, these heat-sealed boots are crafted with a lightly cushioned leather and insole for all-day comfort.
