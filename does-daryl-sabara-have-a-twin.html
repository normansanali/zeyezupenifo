Does Daryl Sabara have a twin?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, October 2, 2024

Daryl Sabara has a twin. He was raised alongside a fraternal twin brother, Evan Sabara, who is also a voice actor.

Daryl Sabara was born on June 14, 1992, in Torrance, California, the United States of America. His mother is Sandy Sabara.

Daryl Sabara is of Russian-Jewish descent from his mother and Polish descent from his father. Daryl Sabara graduated from West Torrance High School in 2010.

Daryl Christopher Sabara is an American actor best known for his excellent acting as Juni Cortez in the Spy Kids film series.

Daryl Sabara has made notable appearances in The Polar Express, Wizards of Waverly Place, Father of the Pride, Keeping Up with the Steins, Halloween, Green Inferno, World’s Greatest Dad, Grimm, America’s Most Talented Kids (as a judge), and Weeds.

Daryl Sabara is the spouse of the iconic American singer-songwriter and television personality Meghan Trainor. Daryl Sabara and Meghan Trainor tied the knot on December 22, 2018, Trainor’s 25th birthday.
