Emily Ratajkowski's White Minidress Is Covered in Veggies

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 31, 2024

The little white dress has long been a summer staple, but this Monday, Emily Ratajkowski put a quirky spin on her mini—and nodded to last year’s trending tomato-girl summer aesthetic.

The model stepped out for a shopping day in New York City with a friend, wearing a slinky white minidress in a bright print that featured abstract images of canned tomatoes in a supermarket aisle. “Miaou State Tomatoes,” a sticker print read.

The unique piece hails from Miau—a French brand inspired by the Southern California coastline—and retails for $195. The label’s new collection also includes swimsuits and everyday staples in the fun print.

Gotham//Getty Images

Ratajkowski paired the dress with vibrant green Reebok Club C sneakers, a light brown leather shoulder bag, butter-yellow cat-eye sunglasses with dark lenses, and some necklaces.

More From EmRata

While EmRata’s look is more surfer girl than tomato girl (a vibe that embraces a romantic, laid-back Mediterranean lifestyle), we can’t help but be drawn to the rise in produce-themed fashion. Remember earlier this year, when Emily Blunt hit the red carpet for the Paris premiere of The Fall Guy in a silky vegetable-print set? The glamorous white two-piece was pulled straight from the Loewe Fall/Winter ’24 ready-to-wear collection, and the print included images of red turnips and what appeared to be little potatoes.

If these celebrities have their say, we may not only be eating our veggies this summer, but also wearing them.
