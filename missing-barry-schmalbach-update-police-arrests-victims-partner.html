Missing Barry Schmalbach Update Police Arrests Victims Partner

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, September 26, 2024

Christopher Davis is in custody as Cape Coral Police detain a partner due to an unrelated warrant.

U.S. Cape Coral – The missing person’s partner was recently detained by the Cape Coral Police Department (CCPD) in connection with an unrelated warrant. On August 1, 35-year-old Christopher Davis was apprehended and lodged in the Lee County Jail. According to official court documents, this action was conducted in response to a 2018 South Carolina warrant accusing Davis of violating probation related to a theft offense.

According to the police report, Davis was apprehended outside a neighborhood gym in Cape Coral. It’s crucial to note, however, that Barry Schmalbach, who was 56 years old when Davis vanished, has not been connected to any criminal claims.

While pursuing its efforts to find Barry Schmalbach, the Cape Coral Police Department is actively addressing this issue. Despite having no connection to Schmalbach’s disappearance, this detention highlights the police department’s dedication to following the law and keeping the public safe.
