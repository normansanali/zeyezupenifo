Jeni Britton Bauer Biography

Jeni Britton Bauer is an American entrepreneur, Businesswoman, Author and founder of Jeni’s Splendid Ice Creams. She was born on September 29, 1973 in the United States of America. She is 49 years old as of 2022 and her Zodiac sign is Libra. She is famously known for having founded the Columbus, Ohio based company Jeni’s Splendid Ice Creams.

She and her company have won two Specialty Food Association, Inc. awards. She was a student at Ohio State University and opened her first ice cream shop in 1996. She has also been nominated for Goodreads Choice Awards Best Food & Cookbooks and James Beard Award for Desserts.

Jeni’s has over 60 branded ‘scoop shops’ and retail distributors nationally. Jeni’s won a sofi Gold Award in the “Outstanding Product Line” category. Fast Company named Jeni B one of the Most Creative People in Business in 2018.
