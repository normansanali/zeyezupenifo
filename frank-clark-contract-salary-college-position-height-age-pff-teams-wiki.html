Frank Clark is an American football defensive end who plays in the National Football League for the Kansas City Chiefs. Prior to joining the Kansas City Chiefs, Frank Clark played for the Seattle Seahawks from 2015 to 2018.

Frank Clark was a utility player in high school and he has established himself as one of the top defensive ends in the National Football League.
