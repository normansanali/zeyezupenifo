Remember the Banana and Sprite do to you. All You Need to know

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, July 14, 2024

Remember the Banana and Sprite do to you. All You Need to know-Drinking Sprite and eating a banana together can cause gastric distress due to the carbonation in Sprite and the high levels of potassium in bananas. This may lead to bloating, discomfort, and vomiting.

Is it good to eat bananas with Sprite?

Everyone has their opinions on whether it good to eat bananas with Sprite. Some believe because of the carbonic and potassium reaction; it isn’t good for the body so it isn’t advisable.

What does Sprite and banana Challenge do?

The latest viral banana and Sprite challenge has participants eating two bananas and immediately after that, chugging a bottle of the popular aerated drink. The participant who after taking this together and not vomiting is a winner.

What does coke and banana do?

These two combined will create a gas in the stomach and this may lead to severe pains in the upper quadrant of the stomach.

What does eating 2 bananas and Sprite do?

When someone eats two bananas with Sprite, it generates a gas due to the carbonic content in the Sprite and also the potassium content in the banana. Due to this, one is expected to vomit after making these combinations.

Can you drink Sprite after eating a banana?

Yes, but that should and must be 30 minutes to an hour after eating the banana.
