Marie Avgeropoulos is a Canadian famous for her acting career. She got a breakthrough in her career when she landed the role in the 2014 sci-fi post-apocalyptic tv series, The 100. She gained quite some fame for her portrayal of Octavia Blake.

She will be seen in upcoming films, Butterfly in the Typewriter, Super Dicks, and the action thriller film, King of Killers.
