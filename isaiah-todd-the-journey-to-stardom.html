Isaiah Todd: Bigger Than Basketball

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, July 11, 2024

Isaiah Todd, a rising superstar in the realm of basketball, has been carving an impressive path to greatness. This article delves into his journey, highlighting his development, achievements, and potential.

The Beginning 

Born in 2001, Isaiah Todd was quick to make his mark in the world of basketball. His tremendous potential was evident early on, with his skills and talent being honed from a young age. Todd's journey began in Baltimore, but he later moved to Richmond, Virginia, where he joined John Marshall High School as a freshman. His performances at the high school level made him a hot prospect for top college basketball programs.

In search of better opportunities and exposure, Todd transferred from John Marshall to Trinity Christian, a private school in Raleigh, North Carolina. This move was not driven by a desire for fame or prestige, but rather a dedicated focus on improving his skills and preparing for a professional career. His decision to relocate was also influenced by the presence of his personal trainer, Drew Hanlen, who worked out with Todd in the school's gymnasium.

Isaiah Todd's journey is nothing short of remarkable and inspirational. Despite the challenges he faced growing up, with a mother who was a convicted drug dealer and had to work multiple jobs to support their family, Isaiah became the rock that his family depended on. It was through his exceptional talent and dedication to basketball that he was able to turn their lives around. As a young child in Baltimore, Isaiah was a gifted athlete and a skilled writer. Although he wasn't popular amongst his peers, his talent was discovered on a playground where he was playing outside.

"I had to perform to Survive' - Isaiah Todd

Recognizing his potential, a team approached him to play for AAU and high school basketball. It was at the age of 13 that Isaiah started taking basketball seriously and honing his skills. His relentless determination and hard work paid off, propelling him to become the number one player in the country. Isaiah Todd's story serves as an inspiration to all, reminding us that with passion, perseverance, and the right opportunities, we can overcome any obstacles and achieve greatness.

High School Success

Todd's high school career was replete with success. Standing at an impressive 6-foot-10, he was a dominant force on the court, dazzling audiences and scouts with his agility and precision. He led John Marshall to a state championship in his sophomore year, further cementing his status as a top prospect. As a top 25 Prospects in the World, Todd would receive offers from all the top schools in the nation including Michigan, Kansas, Maryland and more!

Trust the Process

Despite being a high-profile player, Todd remained committed to his development and constantly worked on his game. His rigorous training schedule, combined with his dedication and passion for the sport, laid the groundwork for his future success. He made use of every available resource to hone his skills, displaying a level of maturity and professionalism that belied his young age. He would later commit to play college basketball at Michigan before decommitting to play in the newly formed G-League Ignite.

Entering the G League

In a bold and significant move, Todd chose to bypass college and sign a professional contract with the NBA's G League. This decision was part of the NBA's new initiative to train the nation's top players. Todd was the first player to announce a G League contract, followed by Jalen Green, the top-ranked prospect in the country according to ESPN. Green publicly announced 1st to help get more players signed to G-League Ignite according to Isaiah Todd.

G League Performance

Todd's performance in the G League was nothing short of impressive. He demonstrated a mature shooting ability, holding his own against more seasoned, professional players. His length and fluid shooting stroke made him a formidable offensive threat, while his decision-making and passing abilities were also lauded.

Strengths and Skills

An analysis of Todd's game reveals a player with myriad strengths. His shooting ability is one of his most significant assets, marked by a comfortable catch-and-shoot three-point prowess. He also displays an underrated post-up skill, showing the ability to execute high-arcing turnaround jumpers. His athleticism, combined with his size and skill set, makes him an intriguing prospect for NBA scouts.

NBA Draft and Future Prospects

Signing with the G-League Ignite turned out to be a true blessing for Isaiah Todd, especially considering the uncertainty surrounding the COVID-19 pandemic. Initially, he was not on anyone's draft board and had limited exposure to NBA scouts and teams. However, fate intervened when Isaiah hit a remarkable buzzer-beater that propelled his G-League team into the playoffs.

This clutch performance caught the attention of the basketball world and suddenly put him on the radar of NBA teams. The Milwaukee Bucks recognized his talent and potential, ultimately selecting him 31st overall in the draft. It is truly remarkable how a single shot changed the course of Isaiah's career, turning him into a highly sought-after prospect. This series of events highlights the power of perseverance and seizing opportunities when they arise, even amidst challenging circumstances.

Todd was drafted into the NBA as part of the blockbuster Bradley Beal trade, which saw him joining the Phoenix Suns. Although his stay with the Suns was short-lived, his move to the Memphis Grizzlies presents an exciting opportunity for the young talent. The trade, however, should not be seen as a setback but rather as a stepping stone in his journey to greatness.

The Artist

Isaiah Todd is not just a basketball player, but also a talented artist and song writer. From the very beginning, he never stopped being a writer, using it as an escape and a means of expression. In order to survive, he used music and poetry as an outlet. Today, he can proudly call himself a songwriter and a spoken word artist. For years, he kept his artistic side hidden, but the passion for both basketball and music continued to grow within him.

Isaiah remains a poet at heart, drawing inspiration from fellow hoopers who have successfully broken into the music scene, such as Miles Bridges, Dame Lillard, and Bones Hyland. He firmly believes that this fusion of basketball and music will continue to be a part of the culture, and he anticipates that something great will come from it in the future. Isaiah Todd is a true testament to the power of embracing multiple passions and letting creativity flourish in different forms.

The Road Ahead

Isaiah Todd's journey is far from over. With his tremendous potential and relentless work ethic, he has all the makings of a future NBA star. His progress will be keenly followed by basketball fans and pundits alike, as he continues to develop his skills and make his mark in professional basketball.

A Testament

Isaiah Todd's journey is a testament to his dedication, hard work, and sheer talent. Despite the challenges and changes he has faced, his focus on development and improvement has remained unwavering. As he continues to make strides in his career, there's no doubt that Todd will leave an indelible mark on the world of basketball.

The Takeaway

Isaiah Todd represents the next generation of basketball greats. His journey is a compelling narrative of hard work, talent, and determination. As he continues to grow and develop, his story serves as an inspiration to aspiring athletes everywhere. His success serves as a reminder that with dedication, commitment, and the right opportunities, anything is possible.
