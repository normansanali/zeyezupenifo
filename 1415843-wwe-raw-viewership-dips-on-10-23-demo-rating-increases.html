WWE RAW Viewership Dips On 10/23, Demo Rating Increases

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, July 30, 2024

WWE RAW viewership dropped on October 23, but the key demographic rating increased.

Per Wrestlenomics, an average of 1.476 million viewers watched the show on Monday. This number is slightly down from last week, when RAW averaged 1.483 million viewers

In the 18 to 49 demographic, RAW scored a 0.46 rating. This number marks an increase from last week (0.44 rating in the same demographic).

WWE RAW was up against a Monday Night Football Game between the Minnesota Vikings and the San Francisco 49ers. Plus, the show competed with the seventh game of the American League Championship Series between the Texas Rangers and the Houston Astros.

What Happened On This Week’s Episode Of WWE RAW?

The October 23 episode of WWE RAW featured a singles match between Damian Priest and Jey Uso. Plus, Drew McIntyre battled Sami Zayn. Elsewhere on the show, Becky Lynch defended the NXT Women’s Championship against Indi Hartwell.
