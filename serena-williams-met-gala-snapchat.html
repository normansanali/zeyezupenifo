Pregnant Serena Williams Dances Around the Met Gala

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, August 2, 2024

While your favorite celebrities were dancing it out and smoking cigarettes in the bathroom at the Met Gala, Serena Williams was wandering the halls of the museum like a princess.

Not long after giving her baby bump its long-awaited red carpet debut, the tennis star was seen frolicking, leaping, twirling and dancing through the halls of the Met. Luckily, she posted the highlights of the performance—which she fittingly titled "Night at the museuam"—on social media for the world to see. From the looks of the photos and videos, the tennis champ was really feeling herself, and we don't blame her—especially in that flowing green Versace dress and unmistakeable pregnancy glow.
