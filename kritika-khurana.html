Wiki/Biography

Kritika was born on Saturday, 19 June 1993 (age 29 years; as of 2022) in New Delhi. Her zodiac sign is Gemini. She studied non-medical from St. Lawrence Convent School, New Delhi. Later, she did her graduation in Fashion & Design from JD Institute of London. [1] [2] [3] [4]

Career

She started her career as a social media influencer and used to post OOTD- Outfit of the day pictures on Instagram, which got immensely popular in 2013 and 2014. She then started working as a blogger with her blog ‘ThatBohoGirl,’ where she wrote stories about fashion and styling tips. She has her own online clothing store ‘The Hype’ which was started in 2014. After getting popular as a blogger, she started her YouTube channel Kritika Khurana and uploaded her first video ‘Styling Sneakers’ in 2014. She has uploaded vlogs and various videos on her YouTube channel related to fashion, styling, and motivation.

Facts/Trivia

  • While she was in school, she wanted to become an architect.
  • She has won many awards in Indian classical dance forms while she was in school and college.
  • In an interview, when she was asked how she developed an interest in fashion, she said,

Being an introvert, I always loved fashion, as a medium for expressing myself through styling. I always had an eye for fashion as, whenever I went shopping, I used to get stuck on one particular outfit and imagine myself wearing it in several different ways. I have always loved the idea of playing with different silhouettes, fabrics etc and being a different person daily.”

  • In an interview in 2019, Kritika disclosed about suffering from anxiety and depression. She said,

When I was in the 10th grade, I had a major fall out with my best friend. Overnight I became lonely. It felt like my world had turned upside down. Problems look much bigger & things hurt more when you’re just a child. So to cope, I began to binge eat. I became an overweight kid who was always in the background. I even started losing weight & lost almost 25 kilos! Now that I had a platform, I decided to talk about something I wished I knew when I was a kid – self-love. I wanted to reach out to anybody who’d been through what I did & be there for them.”
