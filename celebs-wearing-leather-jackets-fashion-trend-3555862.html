Gigi Hadid &amp; More Try The Trend Hollywood Life

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, September 28, 2024

The last time a leather trench coat was popular was when The Matrix came out and it was Neo’s signature outfit. Now, however, some of our favorite top models and stars are putting a chic twist on the jacket. Hailey Baldwin tried out the trend on May 11 when she rocked an oversized black leather Magda Butrym Winter 2021 Coat with a pair of baggy white Materiel Linen Trousers, a cropped white T-shirt, Linda Farrow x the Attico Irene Sunglasses, a Prada Brushed Leather Bag, Jennifer Fisher Samira Huggies Earrings, and Adidas Continental 80 Sneakers.

Hailey’s BFF, Kendall Jenner looked just as fabulous when she tried out the trend in NYC on Feb. 15. Kendall looked edgy and chic when she rocked a floor-length black leather Materiel Leather Cut out Coat which she chose to leave open, showing off her toned abs in a black crop top. She styled her shirt with a pair of black I.Am.Gia Black Halo Pants, a Louis Vuitton Pouchette Eva Bag, white Nike Air Force 1 Sneakers, and black Kim Kardashian West x Carolina Lemke Dusk Sunglasses.

Kourtney Kardashian has been absolutely loving the trend and one of our favorite looks was when she stepped out for dinner at Dan Tana’s restaurant in West Hollywood on Feb. 11 when she looked chic in her ensemble. Kourt threw on an oversized brown tan leather trench coat which was cinched in at the waist with a tight belt tied around her. Under the jacket, she rocked a pair of fitted and flared gray jeans, a lace black top, and a pair of pointed-toe booties. Aside from this look, she rocked the trend again, when she stepped out on Jan. 24 in not one but two outfits, both involving leather trenches. Her first look was a light tan Frame Leather Trench Coat on top of black skinny jeans, black leather The Row Zipped Boots, a Fendi Zucca Mini Pochette Bag, and a pair of Port Tanger Leila Acetate Sunglasses. Meanwhile, later that day, she rocked a pair of high-waisted brown Nanushka Marfa Jeans with a plunging V-neck brown satin bodysuit tucked in. On top of her outfit, she threw on a dark brown Theory Varnished Leather Coat in Dark Roast, which she chose to keep open. She topped her look off with the same sunglasses from earlier in the day and a pair of black Celine Madame Boots.

Emily Ratajkowski also tried out the look when she was out in New York City on Dec. 28. She looked cozy and chic in a black patent leather Loewe Belted Shearling Coat with a fluffy white collar and a belt that cinched in her tiny waist. Emily styled the coat with a pair of gray sweatpants, a cream Orseund Iris Cropped Ribbed-Knit Turtleneck Sweater, Reality Xray Specs in Turtle, and a pair of Stella McCartney Ultra Boost Sneakers.

Another one of our favorite models who has tried the trend was Kaia Gerber who has been rocking leather jackets a ton recently, but especially her trench coat. She stepped out in NYC on Oct. 13, when she wore a pair of baggy high-waisted blue jeans with a black sweater, a black Danielle Guizo Belted Leather Trench Coat, white Cactus Plant Flea Market x Nike Air Force 1 Low sneakers, and a ’47 New York Yankees Hat.

Basically, all of the Kardashian/Jenner sisters have worn a leather trench recently, in their own unique way. Kim Kardashian, was spotted in an all-black ensemble of skin-tight, high-waisted shiny Yeezy pants with a long-sleeve t-shirt tucked in. The star of her outfit, though, was her fabulous long, thin black Gucci by Tom Ford coat which she paired with Balenciaga Knife Ankle Boots.

Gigi Hadid is also a huge fan of the look. Gigi looked unbelievably chic when she opted to wear an extra-long reddish-brown leather Tommy Hilfiger jacket that zipped up the front and had a thick belt that cinched in her petite waist. She kept the bodice of the jacket open to show off a bunch of layered necklaces and topped the look, off with a matching pair of leather slouchy boots.
