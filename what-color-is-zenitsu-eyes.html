How did Zenitsu get his hair yellow?

Zenitsu's hair turned yellow because he was struck by lightning. He was hiding in a tree during a training session with the Thunder Hashira, Jigoro Kuwajima, when lightning struck the tree and changed Zenitsu's hair from black to yellow.

Why is Zenitsu only strong when sleeping?

Zenitsu, while awake, is aware of his surroundings, and can sense demons quite efficiently with his 'Enhanced Sense of Hearing', but when it comes to even slight contact with a demon, he blacks out. So, to sum it up, his fear of going against terrifying demons that could end his life, drives him to fall asleep.
