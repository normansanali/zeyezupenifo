John Cena Shares Thoughts on WWE and UFC Merger Deal Reaching Its Conclusion

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, September 15, 2024

John Cena is a brand of his own and his name continues to be high value no matter what. The Leader of Cenation might have adopted a part-timer role over the last six years, but he is now on the longest run during his current stint in WWE. He is obviously keeping up with what goes on in WWE and that includes WWE and UFC’s merger deal getting finalized after months. Now it appears Cena reacted to the deal’s finalization.

As previously reported, the new name of the publicly traded company as result of WWE and UFC’s merger would be TKO Group Holdings. WWE and UFC’s deal was also finalized just today, as the companies were featured in front of The New York Stock Exchange.

The 16-time World Champion took to Twitter and reacted to the news of WWE and UFC’s merger deal being finalized, as he called the move the evolution in the sports-entertainment industry.

Another historic moment in the evolution of the sports-entertainment industry. Congratulations to everyone involved in today’s massive announcement. @Endeavor @WWE @UFC

Change is the only constant in life and WWE has accepted the upcoming changes as a result of this historic merger with the UFC. Only time will tell whether WWE will see more improvements as a result of the merger.
