Money Oxhorn Biography And Net Worth

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 3, 2024

Money Oxhorn is a famous American lifestyle YouTube channel created and hosted by Brandon Dennis. Unlike many channels with a plan and format, Brandon Dennis just creates video content about anything he feels like posting or is interested in and that includes a wide range of topics like sports, pranks, challenges, and life hacks.

The channel has over 1.5 million subscribers as of 2021 and has accumulated over 600 million views so far. It is able to get an average of 260,000 views per day from different sources.

Brandon Dennis popularly known as Money Oxhorn is an author, game commentator, former machinima artist, and host of the live stream show Scotch & Smoke Rings. Brandon Dennis towers at 6 feet 2 inches tall and weighs 80kg with a very thick dark beard, dark hair, and blue eyes. Although famous, not much is known about his early childhood.

Brandon Dennis Net Worth

Brandon Dennis has an estimated net worth of $1.7 million dollars. Brandon accumulated significant portions of his money from gains from advertisements on his YouTube channel.
