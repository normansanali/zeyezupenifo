Sources Say Diddy Saw Women As 'Things' After Ex-Girlfriend's Death Traumatized Him

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Highlights

  • Diddy's behavior changed after ex Kim Porter's death, with reports suggesting he lost respect for women and people around him.
  • Grief over Kim Porter's death may have impacted Diddy, but people have shared stories of his questionable behavior pre-2018.
  • Celebrities have spoken out against Diddy, cautioning not to be surprised by the recent allegations against him.

After the shocking raids on Diddy Combs' houses in Los Angeles and Miami, disturbing facts about the rapper's life and behavior have come to light, with people who know him sharing their experiences with him, and celebrities distancing themselves from the rapper.

Inside Diddy's Shocking Allegations Following His Historic House Raids

There's a disturbing reason why P. Diddy's Miami and Los Angeles homes were raided in March 2024.

A new report came out, which doesn't paint Diddy in a good light, but attempts to offer some explanations into why his behavior became so erratic. According to new sources, it was the death of the love of his life, Kim Porter, that led to the rapper's questionable treatment of the people around him. Especially women.

Diddy's Attitude Toward Women Reportedly Changed When His Ex Died

Amid the scandal surrounding Diddy Combs, new reports have come up explaining the rapper's downfall. Apparently, it all started when his ex-girlfriend and mother of his children, Kim Porter, passed away. According to new reports, it changed his attitude toward women completely, and he lost respect for people as he saw that no one could compare to her.

"When Kim died, it rocked Diddy's world," an insider said to Us Weekly. "It was a massive shock and a turning point. All the other women in his life were playthings. Kim was the real deal. It was all down from there."

Of course, grief is no excuse to treat anyone like a "plaything", and far from helping Diddy's case, this admission really shocked people. It is true, however, that the rapper was in pieces when Kim Porter died, and it surely changed something in him.

"For the last three days I’ve been trying to wake up out of this nightmare. But I haven’t," Diddy wrote shortly after she died. "I don’t know what I’m going to do without you, baby. I miss you so much. Today I’m going to pay tribute to you. I’m going to try and find the words to explain our unexplainable relationship. We were more than best friends, we were more than soulmates. WE WERE SOME OTHER S**T!! And I miss you so much. Super Black Love."

Diddy's Questionable Behavior Allegedly Started Long Before Kim Porter's Death

While Kim Porter's passing probably marked a turning point in Diddy's life, many celebrities have spoken about the rapper's questionable, and sometimes dangerous, behavior. And the stories they shared allegedly took place long before 2018, the year his ex died.

Former "Extra" host Tanika Ray, who was a backup dancer for the rapper in the 90s, said she had a "horrific" experience with him, which she was too scared to even speak aloud.

Related
Aubrey O'Day Says Former Boss Sean 'Diddy' Combs' Lawsuit Came 'Too Late'

While the news that Diddy Combs had his houses raided might have been a shock to a lot of people, it wasn't to Aubrey O'Day.

"I just knew to avoid him at all costs," she said. "Yes, I danced for him and kept my space. I was on the Vote or die airplane and kept my space. I interviewed him for his projects and kept my space." She also added that people shouldn't be shocked by the recent allegations. "Nothing that is happening is surprising," she stated. "Shame on all those men that let this continue."

The lawsuits against him are still in process, and it's too early to tell what the consequences will be, but things aren't looking well for the rapper.
