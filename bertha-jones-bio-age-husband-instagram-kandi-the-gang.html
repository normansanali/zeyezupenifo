Meet Kandi &amp; The Gang's Bertha Jones! Her Bio, Age, Husband, Instagram

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

Meet Bertha Jones, the aunt of Kandi Burruss from BravoTV’s “Kandi & The Gang” and also the show star. Her outspoken nature has made her a fan favorite. Learn more about her age and her job in this article.

Tag along with this Bertha Jones Bio as it brings you more information on Bertha.

Bertha Jones On Kandi & The Gang

“Kandi & The Gang” is a new BravoTV reality show with a similar theme to “Vanderpump Rules.” However, it differs from the latter show in one area: gay guys. The show follows the controversy at Atlanta-based eatery Old Lady Gang (OLG), whose owner is Kandi Burruss of the Real Housewives of Atlanta.

The new show has been the talk of the town for its peculiar nature of including members of the LGBTQ+ community as a cast who also happens to be Kandi’s employees.

“When I realized that we did something different than anybody else was when the commercial [for ‘Kandi & the Gang] came out,” speaking about the show Kandi told PageSix.

“I saw people in the comments saying, ‘Wow, this is dope that they are showing this and I feel included now.’ I was like, ‘Wow,’” Kandi added. “We all exist, right? But we’re not necessarily all represented when it comes to television. So come on to ‘Kandi & The Gang.’ We’re going to make everybody feel right at home.”

Her Bravo bio reads, “Namesakes of the restaurant, the “Old Lady Gang” work at OLG and keep a watchful eye over the staff’s lives. Kandi’s mom, Mama Joyce, is the life of the party with all the tea, Aunt Nora is feisty but loving, and Aunt Bertha is the side-eye queen of Atlanta! The ladies are strong, opinionated, and speak their mind.”

Bertha Jones has joined her family and staff from her restaurant and launched “Kandi & The Gang” which has been well-received for its raw component.

Bertha Jones Age

In November 2021, Bertha Jones turned 80 years old. She celebrates her birthday on 17 November.

Does Bertha Jones Have A Husband?

Bertha Jones, unlike her sister Nora, doesn’t seem to have a husband. But, she has a large family all of them are very well connected. Bertha has at least two daughters Kim Payne (@kimpayne52), Joyce Dallas (@cousinwennie). Kim celebrates her birthday on 25 September.

Kim’s daughter and Bertha’s granddaughter is Kandice Butler (@justkandy1). Whereas Joyce has a son named Patrick Dallas (@kightpatrick_) who is also the star in the reality show and a daughter named Cece Raven (@realtorcece). Patrick is engaged to Safari Foxe (@safarifoxe).

Then Bertha has Kenneth Butler Sr. (@stonergawd1), her first grandson. He has a son named Kenneth Jr. who turned 15 in 2021. Moreover, he also seems to have other two children who appear on his IG.

Bertha Jones Net Worth

Bertha Jones has stacked a net worth under $300 thousand.

She is one of the three OGs of “Old Lady Gang” to who Kandi credits teaching her the ropes in the kitchen. She and her two sisters Nora Wilcox and Kandi’s mom Joyce Jones are the faces of the restaurant. The restaurant was conceptualized in 2016 by her niece Kandi and her husband Todd Tucker.

The restaurant is currently based in the Castleberry Hill neighborhood in Atlanta, GA. According to the restaurant’s website, “Joyce Jones, Bertha Jones & Nora Wilcox are the original OLG. If there’s one thing that you don’t have to question, when it comes to these three firecrackers, is that family truly comes first as far as they’re concerned. They’re three enthusiastic sisters from Atlanta that certainly know their way around a kitchen.”

“One thing is for certain, if these three women put as much love and dedication into their original recipes as they do into their families, we are guaranteed to have some of the best southern cuisines that the South has to offer.”

Bertha has an IMDB account. From 2009 to 2021, Bertha appeared on 10 episodes of The Real Housewives of Atlanta. She also appeared on RHOA: Kandi’s Ski Trip in 2015.

As for her job, Bertha Jones might’ve once worked as a police officer or security.
