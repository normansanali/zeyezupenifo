Debbie Matenopoulos Gives Birth, Welcomes Daughter Alexandra Kalliope

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

Matenopoulos is a mama! Home & Family host Debbie Matenopoulos and her husband Jon Falcone have welcomed their first child, a source confirms to Us Weekly.

"Overjoyed to welcome Alexandra Kalliope Falcone into the world!" Matenopoulos tweeted about her baby girl on Thursday, Oct. 30. "7 lbs, 1 oz."

Matenopoulos, 39, revealed in April that she was both pregnant and had secretly wed Falcone last summer. 

"I feel so blessed that this is happening to me," the former View co-host told Us at the time. "Everything in life is truly beautiful."

"I eloped in Greece and am four months pregnant," she added. "It has been a great journey so far."

In anticipation of her baby's arrival, Matenopoulos was the guest of honor at a baby shower on Sept. 7. She celebrated at a friend's home in L.A.'s Brentwood neighborhood and was feted by Lisa Ling, Holly Robinson Peete, Brooke Anderson, Vivica A. Fox, and former Real Housewives of New York City star Jill Zarin.

This is the first child for Matenopoulos. She was previously married to L.A. music executive Jay Faires from 2003 to 2009.
