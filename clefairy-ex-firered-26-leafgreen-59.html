Clefairy (EX FireRed &amp; LeafGreen 59)

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, July 5, 2024

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Clefairy  
ピッピ Pippi
For more information on this Pokémon's species, see Clefairy.

Clefairy (Japanese: ピッピ Pippi) is a Colorless-type Basic Pokémon card. It is part of the EX FireRed & LeafGreen expansion.

Card text

Gesture
このゆびとまれ
Choose 1 of your opponent's Benched Pokémon and switch it with the Defending Pokémon. Your opponent chooses the Defending Pokémon to switch.
Moon Kick
ムーンキック		20

Trivia

Origin

Gesture's Japanese name is that of Follow Me, a move in the Pokémon games that Clefairy can learn. Moon Kick is an attack that first appeared on Erika's Clefairy from Gym Challenge.
