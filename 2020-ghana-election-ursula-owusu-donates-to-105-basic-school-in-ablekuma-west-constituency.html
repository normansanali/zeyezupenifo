2020 Ghana Election: Ursula Owusu Donates To 105 Basic School In Ablekuma West Constituency

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Minister for Communication Honorable Ursula Owusu on Thursday 10 September, 2020, made a donation to 105 basic schools in Ablekuma West Constituency where she serves as their representative in Parliament

The donation comprises of 3000 momo desk and 2000 mathematical sets.

Madam Ursula says she believe her donation will have great impact on the beneficiary schools especially, the final year students in the various Junior High Schools she presented the donation to.

She said “This donation i am optimistic would help final year JHS students in the constituency to successfully write the upcoming Basic Education Certificate Examination without stress.”
