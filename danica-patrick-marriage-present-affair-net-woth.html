What is Danica Patrick’s Marital status?

Danica Patrick is now again single. Last time, she has been dating Carter Comstock, the co-founder of Freshly Inc. The former lovebirds went Instagram official last year, less than one year after her split from Rodgers.

Unfortunately, their relationship didn’t long last and confirmed the split in March 2022. Who is Danica Patrick previously dating? Let’s get to know more about her past affairs.

Paul Edward Hospenthal

Danica has always been open about her love life and all its complications. The former NASCAR racer first went public with her romance with the physical therapist, Paul Edward Hospenthal.

The pair were married from 2005 to 2012. The two initially met in 2002 after visiting his office for a knee injury that she suffered during a yoga session. However, their divorce was just finalized in April 2013.

Ricky Stenhouse Jr.

After a split with the physical therapist, she started dating the NASCAR star Ricky Stenhouse Jr. in the same year. They had an affair from November 2012 to December 2017.

Patrick once expressed her desire to get married and have kids with the race car driver after the separation. She told,

“The relationship has really evolved into this really comfortable space where we’re … just really happy. It’s really straightforward and simple. And we don’t argue.”

Was Aaron Rodgers in a relationship with Danica Patrick?

In January 2018, Patrick’s relationship with the NFL player Aaron Rodgers was confirmed after months of speculation. The two first met at the ESPY Awards in 2012.

Patrick told US magazine that she could always count on Rodgers to have her back. She said,

“You can rely on yourself or you can also rely on those closest to you that are honest. Not people that want to tell you what you want to hear, but people that are gonna tell you what you need to hear and be honest with you,”

Unfortunately, their love life didn’t long last and split their ways in July 2020.
