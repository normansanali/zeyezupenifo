Why is the Accident Report Important?

The accident report, often prepared by law enforcement at the scene of the accident, contains crucial information about the incident. It includes details such as the date, time, and location of the accident, the parties involved, witness statements, and any citations issued. This report serves as an official record of the accident and can significantly impact the outcome of your claim or insurance proceedings.
