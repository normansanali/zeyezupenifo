American Actor, Bryan Greenberg Married Actress Jamie Chung in 2015

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, August 8, 2024

Bryan Greenberg is an American actor and singer who gained popularity after portraying as Jake Jagielski in the WB series One Tree Hill from 2003–06 in total 25 episodes. Also, he is famous for his main roles including Nick Garrett on the ABC drama October Road from 2007-08 and as Ben Epstein in the HBO original series How to Make It in America from 2010-11.  

As a singer, Greenberg has released his two ablums named Waiting for Now in 2007 and We Don't Have Forever in 20011. His hits singles include Walk Away, Someday, 36 Hours, Daydream, Busy Bee, California Dream, Something in the Way, etc.

Who is Bryan Greenberg Married?

Forty-two years old, Bryan has been enjoying a blissful marital relationship with his wife, Jamie Chung for five years. She is an actress and former reality TV star who is famous for being the cast member of MTV reality series The Real World: San Diego.

Greenberg met Jamie once at a party in around 2007 and they hit it off. After very respectful and a conversation for a long time, they again met each other in New York and went on date. But after them, they did not talk for a year.

When They Began Dating?

Formally, the lovebirds started dating in Los Angeles in early 2012 and it was kind of a five-year process, Chung said. She continues saying: “It really was like missed opportunities, missed timings, different cities and then finally we ended up in the same city together.”

After nearly 6 years of first met and one year of love romance, they announced their engagement in December 2013. One Tree Hill actor popped the question to his fiancee at the Hotel Vitale during a visit to her hometown of San Jame. Before getting down on his knee, he sang a song he'd written about them.

Lavish Outdoor Wedding Ceremony

After three years of dating, Bryan, 42, and Jamie, 33, finally became husband and wife on 31 October 2015. Their exchanged nondenominational vows in a traditional Jewish ceremony at El Capitan Canyon resort in Santa Barbara, California.

They had launched a three-day celebration which consists of a welcome dinner on Halloween Eve surrounded by 243 guests wearing costumes. During their wedlock, the bride Jamie donned two wedding gowns. And the first one was a long-sleeve white gown with a plunging v-neck by Monique Lhuillier.

And the next one was a short lace dress with a front slit and gorgeous lace by Jonathan Simkhai. Whereas the groom Greenberg stuck with one set in the entire ceremony wearing a navy suit, white button-down and black bow tie. 

What is Bryan Greenberg's Net Worth?

Bryan Greenberg has an impressive net worth of $3 million as of 2020 while Chris Tamburello has a net worth of $800,000. He has acquired his fortune throughout his successful acting performances in various hits film and series. His famous TV shows are One Tree Hill, October Road, and How to Make it in America.

As per his salary, Greenberg earned $22,000 per episode for his role Jake Jagielski in One Tree Hill on WB/CW. Actress Danneel Ackles also starred as Rachel Gatina in the show. His film work includes A Civil Action, Prime, Bride Wars, and Friends with Benefits.

His depicted 2001's film Friends with Benefits has grossed over $150.4 million worldwide, against a budget of $35 million. In the movie, he played the character of Parker starring Patricia Clarkson. Apart, he makes an extra sum of amount from his music career.

Bio and Early Life

The 1.83 m tall Bryan was born on May 24, 1978, in Omaha, Nebraska, United States. His parents are Denny Greenberg, a psychologist and mother Carl Greenberg. Further, he has a younger sister Becca Greenberg.

He attended at Beth El Synagogue and later moved to St. Louis, Missouri at age 12. Then he studied at the Parkway Central High School in Chesterfield, Missouri. Later, he received a Bachelor of Arts Degree from New York University and started acting in many theatre productions.
