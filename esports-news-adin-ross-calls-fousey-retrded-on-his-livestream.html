That is what Fousey is known for, Adin Ross calls Fousey ret*rded on his livestream

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, July 25, 2024

Adin Ross recently reacted to Fousey’s comeback to streamline, and called him ‘ret*rded’. Adin and Fousey have a pretty friendly relationship among themselves. 

They have both streamed together before. However, fans have mixed reactions to Ross’s comment on his friend, who constantly suffers from real mental health issues.

Fousey was a very famous YouTuber back in the day before eventually succumbing to his ‘mental issues’. Furthermore, Fousey has gained a significant following on YouTube, with over 10 million subscribers. 

However, he was off the internet for about 4 months due to his mental health issues. The YouTuber was admitted to a mental institution for several weeks before his comeback to streaming.

Fousey returned to streaming on January 2nd, just after New Year’s. He also joined Adin Ross on his stream. Adin has always been kind to Fousey. Furthermore, he also promised to give Fousey 100,000 dollars for his struggles. 

Adin Ross and Fousey reunite after Fousey’s comeback

In Adin’s January 2nd stream on Kick, the streamer was joined by Fousey. Fousey’s return stream gained over 15k viewers. However, the streamer still wasn’t happy with his return, as he claimed ‘he expected better, and his expectations destroyed him’. Furthermore, he added that he could’ve done a lot better and seemed genuinely disappointed.

Ross, on his stream, was trying to help Fousey navigate his comeback by talking to him about it. He asked Fousey to explain what was causing him to be so low. Furthermore, he presses Fousey to ‘talk to him’. 

Fousey then points out that he is having a hard time even talking, and maybe that’s the issue. He also says to Adin that he thinks ‘he flopped’.

I'm gonna be honest bro, you do a lot of r*tarded shit, you are, that's what Fousey is known for bro, so, Fousey, we just want, we don't want fousey with any filter, we want ‘the Fousey’. You still have it in there. I just saw it, now I see the energy and the inspiration.
Ross lamented

Adin quickly disagrees and then tries to help out Fousey by claiming that he needs to get the ‘old Fousey’ back. In the process of motivating Fousey, Adin calls him r*tarded.

However, this doesn’t sit right with the viewers, as they think Adin is pushing him into his habits, and a real friend wouldn’t do that. Some fans also thought that Adin was encouraging him to ‘self-destruct’.
